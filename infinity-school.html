The Modern Shaman Training

Infinity School

Bufo Facilitator Training

A twelve-month Bufo (5-MeO-DMT) facilitator training that bridges the tribal, indigenous way with the modern Western world.

The bridge between worlds

The cosmic and the future

Infinity School is a complete Bufo (Otac / 5-MeO-DMT) facilitator training. It honors ancestral knowledge and cosmovisions while drawing on cultural immersion, scientific discovery, and Western protocols, so that indigenous and Western communities can join as one to help heal and awaken the world to a remembrance of who we are.

Indigenous prophecies foretold a time when the rainbow warriors would learn from the elders and return to a harmonious way of life. Graduates step into that lineage: a community of medicine people, healers, and energy workers. A cosmic tribe.

The 12-month curriculum

A year of infinite knowledge

3 in-person meet-ups

Our shamanic-school curriculum, developed by Héctor Benton Martínez and Hanna Sieranen, unfolds across twelve months of study, ceremony, and integration.

  1. 01

    History & Culture of Bufo Alvarius

    Explore the significance of Bufo Alvarius in indigenous cultures and the teachings it carries.

  2. 02

    Patient Care with Bufo Alvarius 5-MeO

    An operational manual covering the application and care techniques involved in working with the medicine.

  3. 03

    Uses, Customs & Universal Shamanism

    Understanding the practices and beliefs that form the foundation of shamanism across cultures.

  4. 04

    Instruments & Techniques in Cultural Practices

    Examining the tools used in shamanic ritual and the purpose each one serves.

  5. 05

    Spirits of the Wind & Ceremony Control

    Connecting with the elemental spirits and learning to hold and guide ceremony effectively.

  6. 06

    Reflection on Knowledge Gained

    Revisiting and integrating earlier teachings into your current practice.

  7. 07

    Ancestral Wisdom & Healing Practices

    Learning from ancestral healing methods and their relevance today.

  8. 08

    Connection to Nature & the Elements

    The central place of nature in shamanic teaching and practice.

  9. 09

    Rituals & Their Significance

    The meaning of the rituals that hold spiritual and community life.

  10. 10

    The Role of Community in Shamanism

    How community shapes shamanic practice and the way teachings are passed on.

  11. 11

    Integration

    Techniques and practices for integrating shamanic wisdom into personal and communal life.

  12. 12

    Integration into Daily Life

    Practical ways to carry this wisdom and practice into everyday living.

What the training includes

Twelve months, in small groups

Master students meet online three times a week with their master teachers, plus every other Saturday morning for integration. The Infinity Knowledge will open your mind to see the world through many different perspectives.

Students learn holistic healing with natural medicines, plants, and energies; how to connect with the elements, with nature, and with one another; and how to serve 5-MeO-DMT in ancestral ways, safely. The course covers the paperwork involved, common challenges, and how to resolve emotional, physical, and spiritual issues before, during, and after ceremony. Ethical, moral, safety, and legal questions are addressed throughout, along with the most effective ways to support integration.

After twelve months, students may continue taking classes as needed. The Infinity Knowledge is a community of medicine workers and guardians who stay in contact and support one another, so our students are never left to navigate a challenging world alone.

Includes: 12 months of training · small groups · 3 live online classes a week · integration Saturdays · 3 in-person meet-ups · medicine collection · lifelong community
