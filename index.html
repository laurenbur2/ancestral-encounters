The teaching & experience of Bufo (Otac)

Ancestral Encounters is the teaching and experience of Bufo alvarius (Otac) in the indigenous way — while creating a bridge of safety for the Western world. Teaching always comes first: we prepare each person with care, context, and respect, so this sacred medicine is met as the ancestors intended.

Teachings & Journeys

Welcome

A path of remembering

Ancestral Encounters is a community dedicated to keeping our healing traditions alive and sharing them with those who walk this path with respect. Through ceremony, story, and time on the land, we help people return to balance.

Teachings

The lineage, preparation, and meaning of Bufo (Otac) — the knowledge that holds the medicine safely. Teaching comes first, always.

Ceremony

Sacred medicine held in the indigenous way, guided by experienced facilitators with care, reverence, and safety.

Journeys

Guided journeys to the Sonoran homeland of the medicine, in partnership with the native communities who protect it.

Who we are

Carrying the teachings forward

For generations our people have understood healing as a relationship — between body and spirit, between community and the land, between the living and those who came before.

Today we open our doors to share these gifts thoughtfully, so that this wisdom continues to heal and is never lost.
