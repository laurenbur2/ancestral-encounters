The teaching & experience of Bufo (Otac)

Ancestral Encounters bridges the ancient, indigenous way of Bufo alvarius, Otac, with the modern Western world. We teach, guide, and hold sacred-medicine ceremony with safety and reverence, in alliance with the Indigenous Comcaac shamans who have been wisdom keepers of the traditional use of 5-MeO-DMT for generations.

The Modern Shaman Training

Infinity School

The bridge between the ancient, indigenous way and the modern Western world, the cosmic and the ancient future. Infinity School is a twelve-month Bufo (5-MeO-DMT) facilitator training: intensive small-group study in holistic healing, energy and plant work, and serving the medicine safely and ethically, with deep integration. Graduates join a lifelong community of medicine people, a cosmic tribe.

Includes: 12 months of training · small groups · 3 live online classes a week · integration Saturdays · 3 in-person meet-ups · medicine collection · lifelong community

Comcaac Bufo (Otac)

A four-day immersion into the Comcaac culture that has worked with Bufo for generations. Learn the cosmovision and ceremony of the 5-MeO-DMT medicine and receive initiation into spiritual and medicine work, individually or in a small group, with the option to acquire sacred ceremonial tools.

Includes: housing, food, teachings, ceremonial items, and airport transfers
