A path of remembering

Ancestral Encounters is a community dedicated to keeping our healing traditions alive and sharing them with those who walk this path with respect. Through ceremony, story, and time on the land, we help people return to balance.

Who we are

Carrying the teachings forward

For generations our people have understood healing as a relationship — between body and spirit, between community and the land, between the living and those who came before.

Today we open our doors to share these gifts thoughtfully, so that this wisdom continues to heal and is never lost.
