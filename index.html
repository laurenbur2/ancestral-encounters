The teaching & experience of Bufo (Otac)

Ancestral Encounters bridges the ancient, indigenous way of Bufo alvarius — Otac — with the modern Western world. We teach, guide, and hold sacred-medicine ceremony with safety and reverence, in partnership with the Comcaac people who have carried this medicine for generations.
