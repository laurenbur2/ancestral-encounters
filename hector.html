Our leaders

Héctor Benton

MSc Psychology · Comcaac Pericú Tribal Member · Guardian of Bufo Alvarius · Comcaac Master Shaman

Héctor Benton is a deeply respected figure in traditional medicine and holistic health, with more than two decades devoted to the study and practice of indigenous healing. Raised in the heart of Mexico, his connection to his heritage and the land shaped his path as a Medicine Man, integrating ancient wisdom with contemporary healing. As a Master Shaman and Guardian of Bufo Alvarius, he carries an extensive, nuanced understanding of the spiritual and therapeutic dimensions of indigenous medicine.

Journey & expertise

Héctor's path is grounded in a rigorous apprenticeship under renowned shamans from regions across Mexico. Over twenty-five years he immersed himself in the mysteries of traditional medicine, learning not only the medicinal properties of plants but also the spiritual and communal dimensions of healing. His studies span a wide range of methods, from plant-based remedies to ancient spiritual rituals, all in service of holistic well-being.

His approach emphasizes the interconnectedness of mind, body, and spirit. Héctor advocates a comprehensive model of health that integrates physical, emotional, and spiritual restoration, guided by the teachings of his elders and wisdom passed down through generations. In practice, this means ceremonies and healing techniques tailored to each individual.

Sacred ceremonies & the temazcal

Héctor is known for guiding people through transformative experiences in the sacred space of the temazcal, or sweat lodge, a cornerstone of his work. This ancient practice facilitates deep physical, emotional, and spiritual cleansing, opening the way to self-discovery and renewal. Within these ceremonies he draws on ancestral wisdom and the elements of nature to create an environment for healing and introspection.

Beyond the temazcal, Héctor leads workshops, ceremonies, and gatherings that foster an appreciation for indigenous culture and healing. He is dedicated to preserving and sharing the heritage of his people, so the teachings of his ancestors continue to inspire future generations.

Cultural preservation & holistic well-being

Héctor's wider mission is to bridge traditional knowledge and modern society. He advocates a deeper understanding of the natural world and humanity's place within it, emphasizing balance, sustainability, and respect for the environment. His work includes educating others about the healing power of sacred plants and the role of community in mental and physical health.

His blend of traditional knowledge, psychological training, and spiritual guidance makes him a leader for those seeking profound transformation and healing. He helps facilitate personal growth, emotional healing, and spiritual renewal, honoring the wisdom of ancient practice while meeting the needs of the modern world.
