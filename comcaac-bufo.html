4-Day Cultural Immersion

Comcaac Bufo (Otac)

A four-day immersion into the Comcaac culture that has worked with Bufo for generations.

Culture & initiation

Four days inside a living tradition

Spend four full days immersed in the rich Comcaac culture that has worked with Bufo, Otac, for generations. Individually or in a small group, you will learn the beautiful cosmovision and culture surrounding the 5-MeO-DMT medicine, and receive initiation into spiritual and medicine work.

Participants receive the items needed for ceremony. You may also acquire tribal sacred ceremonial tools, with permission to use them, to deepen your own ceremonial practice.

What is included

Everything you need, cared for

Your time with the Comcaac is held with care from the moment you land. Housing, meals, teachings, and the items you will need for ceremony are all provided, along with travel to and from the airport.

Includes: housing · food · teachings · ceremonial items · airport transfers, both ways

$1,800 per participant in a small group of four. Private experiences are available at a higher rate.
