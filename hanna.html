Our leaders

Hanna Sieranen

MSW · Trauma Expert · Somatic Experiencing Practitioner · Psychedelic-Assisted Therapist · Transformation & Integration Coach · Comcaac Medicine Woman · Energy Healer

Hanna Sieranen is an accomplished mental health professional and healer, combining more than two decades of expertise in clinical psychology, social work, trauma therapy, and somatic practice with a deep knowledge of traditional and ancestral healing. Born and raised in Finland, she developed a profound interest in human behavior that led her to advanced studies in psychology, social work, anthropology, and political science in both Finland and the United States. Over the past twenty years she has earned numerous certifications for her innovative work in mental health, specializing in trauma, anxiety, and depression.

As a progressive clinician, Hanna continually sought the most effective and rapid healing methods for her clients. Although she found success within conventional therapeutic systems, she recognized that the modern mental health framework often did not fully meet her clients' needs or align with her own approach to healing. She pursued additional training in psychedelic-assisted therapies and expanded her practice to integrate ancient healing practices from indigenous cultures, blending them with her clinical expertise.

Approach & philosophy

Hanna's approach emphasizes the integration of modern clinical technique with traditional medicine and spiritual practice. Having moved through her own transformative healing journey, she is deeply attuned to the emotional and psychological needs of those seeking alternative paths to recovery. Her work is grounded in compassion, safety, and empowerment, so each person feels supported through every step of their healing.

As a somatic experiencing practitioner and psychedelic-assisted therapist, Hanna helps people safely navigate the emotional and psychological challenges of their journeys. She creates secure, emotionally supportive environments during medicine ceremonies, and is devoted to the integration phase, helping clients process, internalize, and apply the insights they gain to everyday life.

Trauma healing & transformation

Her deep understanding of trauma, paired with her expertise in somatic practice, allows her to address the root causes of emotional and psychological pain. She supports people through difficult, transformative experiences, helping them release old, painful perspectives and embrace healthier patterns of thinking and being. By combining clinical trauma work with traditional medicine and energy healing, Hanna offers a holistic path to transformation, peace, clarity, and resilience.

As a transformation and integration coach, she offers tailored strategies that help clients navigate the post-ceremony process with confidence and clarity.

Holistic & integrated healing

Hanna is committed to an inclusive approach to mental health and wellness that honors both modern and ancestral healing. By merging contemporary therapeutic methods with the wisdom of indigenous healing cultures, she offers clients the chance to heal on multiple levels: physical, emotional, and spiritual. Her work as a Comcaac Medicine Woman deepens her ability to guide people through the spiritual and energetic dimensions of well-being, and her careful integration of sacred medicine offers an additional layer of healing and connection.
