A long weekend on the land

Your own ceremony, held with care

Spend a long weekend on a traditional ranch in the Sonoran desert, among the toads. Collect your own medicine and experience 5-MeO-DMT in the safest, most beautiful way.

Share traditional food and a special ancestral Sonoran sweat ceremony of purification and cosmic connection. Most of all, experience your own powerful Bufo ceremony. Release the past, heal yourself, and take charge of your future.

This experience is meant to empower you and reveal your next steps, so you can renew your life and live more authentically and happily.
