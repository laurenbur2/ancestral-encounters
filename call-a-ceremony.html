A ceremony, brought to you

Held with care, wherever you are

Are you drawn to your own safe, personal 5-MeO-DMT (Bufo) ceremony, in Mexico or somewhere close to home? We host weekend retreats in Mexico and travel anywhere in the world to facilitate private ceremonies, one to one or for a small group you trust.

Each ceremony is held with the same care, safety, and reverence as our work on the land, in alliance with the Comcaac wisdom keepers who have served this medicine for generations.

How it works

From first conversation to integration

Every ceremony begins with a conversation and is shaped around you. Here is the path from reaching out to the days that follow.

1 · Discovery call

We start with a discovery and planning call to understand what you are seeking, answer your questions, and make sure this is the right and safe path for you.

2 · Your setting

We arrange a safe, beautiful, private setting, whether that is a weekend retreat in Mexico or traveling to a place that feels right close to your home.

3 · Ceremony & integration

Your ceremony is held one to one or in a small private group, with grounded preparation beforehand and integration support afterward.
