Our leaders

Dr. Santiago Valenzuela

MD · Guardian of Bufo Alvarius · Medicine Man · 20+ Years in Integrative Medicine

Dr. Santiago Valenzuela is a highly regarded physician and shaman with more than twenty years of experience in both conventional and alternative healing. He specializes in ozone therapy, anti-aging, regenerative medicine, medical cannabis, and natural psychedelics, integrating scientific method with ancestral healing traditions. His work is informed by both his medical training and his deep commitment to the transformative potential of sacred medicines.

A multidimensional approach to healing

Dr. Valenzuela is dedicated to supporting each person in their journey toward physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being. He facilitates transformative healing experiences by combining modern scientific tools with ancient wisdom, guiding individuals to reconnect with their higher selves and the natural world. His work emphasizes balance and alignment between body, mind, and spirit, an integrated approach that has made him a leader in the field of holistic health.

Sacred medicine ceremonies

His work is rooted in the belief that healing is a multidimensional process. He offers a unique healing experience through sacred medicine ceremonies that focus on self-discovery, spiritual connection, and personal transformation. These ceremonies provide a safe, protected space where individuals can embark on a deep, inner journey, held by both medical professionals and master practitioners of the sacred medicines. Through them, Dr. Valenzuela helps people access profound healing and gain clarity in their physical, mental, and spiritual lives.

Bufo Alvarius, with science and tradition

His work with natural psychedelics, particularly the revered Bufo Alvarius, is grounded in both modern scientific research and traditional knowledge, ensuring that each session is held safely and effectively. His approach is designed to guide individuals through transformative experiences, helping them rediscover their purpose, heal past trauma, and foster a deeper connection to themselves and the divine.

Educating & bridging worlds

Dr. Valenzuela devotes significant time to educating the public about the potential of sacred medicines and their integration within modern wellness. Through social media and public speaking, he shares his knowledge, promotes a conscious approach to healing, and encourages a respectful understanding of these ancient practices. By combining science and spirituality, he works to bridge the gap between traditional and modern approaches to health.

In collaboration with Hanna Sieranen

Working alongside Hanna Sieranen, MSW, trauma expert and somatic experiencing practitioner, Dr. Valenzuela offers a comprehensive approach that unites Western therapeutic practice with indigenous wisdom. Hanna's expertise in trauma healing, somatic therapy, and psychedelic-assisted therapy complements his medical background, so that each person receives a well-rounded, effective, and compassionate healing experience. Together they provide a safe, supportive environment for people to explore their emotional and spiritual journeys, with a focus on empowerment, self-healing, and integration at every stage.
